About us

At Aapkaghar, we understand that people everywhere are searching for a home to call their own. We want to make this search as joyful as finally finding the perfect home because we understand that finding a home is much more than an online search!

More intriguing information

A home is a cherished memory that lasts forever, it is where the walls embrace memories, the ceilings shelter love and laughter, where the quiet corners offer a much-needed pause and life itself becomes a reason to celebrate.So to make this journey joyful, we begin by partnering with our customers from the start and being there when it matters the most - right from online search to brokers to home loans to paperwork to finally finding that perfect home. At Aapkaghar, we not only help you search but help you find. we help you find joy.

Apart from a robust search mechanism Aapkaghar also maintains a vast repository of research on a host of property related matters ranging from legal issues to maintenance to facility management to home insurance among many others.
